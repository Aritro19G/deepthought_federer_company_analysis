# DeepThought Federer Company Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes specialty and performance chemical companies in Vadodara to identify “Federer companies” — mid-sized, differentiated manufacturers aligned with DeepThought’s Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

The goal was to filter, evaluate, and score companies systematically based on manufacturing capability, differentiation, growth signals, and strategic relevance.

---

## Methodology

The analysis was conducted in multiple structured stages:

### 1. Raw List Creation
**File:** `1_vadodara_raw_list.csv`  
- Created an initial list of **56 companies** in Vadodara  
- Focused on specialty and performance chemicals  

---

### 2. First-Stage Analysis (Type & Fit Filtering)
**File:** `2_first_stage_analysis.csv`  
- Evaluated companies based on:
  - Manufacturer vs trader  
  - Segment relevance  
  - Basic ICP alignment  

- Eliminated **25 companies** that did not meet the criteria  

---

### 3. First-Stage Elimination Result
**File:** `3_first_stage_elimination_result.csv`  
- Contains the refined list after removing non-relevant companies  

---

### 4. Second-Stage Analysis (Revenue Filtering)
**File:** `4_second_stage_analysis.csv`  
- Further filtered companies based on revenue:
  - Removed companies that were **too large (>₹500Cr)**  
  - Removed companies that were **too small (<₹10Cr with weak signals)**  

- Eliminated **6 additional companies**  

---

### 5. Second-Stage Elimination Result
**File:** `5_second_stage_elimination_result.csv`  
- Final shortlisted set before detailed scoring  

---

### 6. Final Evaluation (Scoring & Qualification)
**File:** `6_final_output.csv`  
- Evaluated **25 companies** across 6 criteria:

  - **C1:** Manufacturer  
  - **C2:** India-based operations  
  - **C3:** Differentiation (products, IP, niche)  
  - **C4:** Technical decision-maker  
  - **C5:** Industry tailwind  
  - **C6:** Growth signals  

- Each company includes:
  - Evidence-backed scoring  
  - Final verdict (**Strong fit / Fit / Borderline**)  
  - Personalized outreach hook  

---

## Key Outcome

A curated list of 25 Vadodara-based chemical companies classified by their fit with DeepThought’s ICP, supported by structured, evidence-based analysis.
